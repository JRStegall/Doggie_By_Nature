Doggie By Nature

Sarah Castro

A  life long animal lover, part-time extreme marathoner and a full-time furbaby mommy to Carla and Sassy, Sarah dedicates as much time and love to her clients and fosters as she does her own animals.

Offering in-home boarding, midday and vacation visits, and overnight stays, Doggie By Nature works hard and takes pride in providing the comfort your pet is accustomed to while giving you the piece of mind you need to focus on the day or just relax and enjoy yourself while away.
